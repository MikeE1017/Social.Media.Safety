Tips for Staying Safe Online

Why Social Media Safety Matters

Social media connects us with friends and family, but it also exposes us to risks like privacy breaches and cyberbullying.

Learning to protect yourself online is essential for maintaining a positive experience and avoiding potential harm.

Top Safety Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Think twice before you share personal information.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
  • Review and update privacy settings regularly to control who sees your posts.

Steps to Protect Your Account:

  1. Update your passwords every few months.
  2. Turn on login alerts to be notified of suspicious logins.
  3. Always log out when using a shared computer or device.
  4. Report fake accounts or suspicious messages immediately.
